What is GoodForIt?

GoodForIt determines someone’s credit risk through their usage history of payment and lending apps like Venmo, Paypal, and Splitwise. Not only can we provide a new market of creditworthy users for financial service providers, we can also provide more access to financial services for people traditionally underserved by them.

We’ve integrated two lending apps—Splitwise and Venmo, which are both pretty effective measurements of whether or not an individual pays off debt, and how quickly.
